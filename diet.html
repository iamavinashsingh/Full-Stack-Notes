Diet Plan You Need :-


  1. Early Morning (6:00 AM – 7:00 AM)

    • Warm water with lemon
    • Soaked almonds (5–6)
    • Optional:
      1. Green tea
      2. Chia seeds water

  2. Breakfast (8:00 AM – 9:00 AM)

    • Oats porridge with fruits
    • Besan chilla with curd
    • Add-ons:
      1. Banana
      2. Handful of walnuts

  3. Lunch (1:00 PM – 2:00 PM)

    • Brown rice or roti (2–3)
    • Mixed veg curry or paneer sabzi
    • Dal or rajma
    • Sides:
      1. Salad (cucumber, carrot, beetroot)
      2. Curd or buttermilk

  4. Pre-Workout Snack (4:30 PM – 5:00 PM)

    • Peanut butter toast
    • Black coffee
    • Banana or dates

  5. Post-Workout Meal (6:30 PM – 7:00 PM)

    • Protein shake
    • Sprouts salad
    • Paneer cubes or boiled chickpeas

  6. Dinner (8:00 PM – 9:00 PM)

    • Khichdi or mixed veg soup
    • Roti with light sabzi
    • Optional:
      1. Glass of warm turmeric milk (before bed)
